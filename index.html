Céline Brand Story

Founded in 2018 by Luisa Grahams, the creative business entrepreneur with an urge to build a brand that is more than a high-quality product but a lifestyle brand that defines its uniqueness through the ongoing excitement between commercial reality and creativity.

Focusing on quality material, good design, craftsmanship and sustainability, Céline reflects the epitome of quality over quantity.

Céline is a digital first, direct-to-consumer brand defining a new era of luxury. Innovation being the key factor alongside aesthetic, Céline has brought unexpected shapes with smart details, functionality and a new luxury feel with a contemporary price point.
