HOW TO CONTACT CÉLINE
Call our representatives or write to Céline

Should you require help or additional information, please contact our Client Services at 27(589) 236 971 where one of our dedicated advisers will be happy to assist you. Alternatively, you may contact them at any time by email using the Contact Us form.

OUR PRODUCTS
Where to find more information about Céline products?

The Celine website presents the entire current collection of handbags, travel bags, fashion jewelry, sunglasses, shoes and ready-to-wear.

Discover the complete Celine product offer by clicking on the COLLECTIONS tab on Celine.com.

Should you require further information about Louis Vuitton products, please do not hesitate to contact Client Services at +1.866.Celine where one of our dedicated client service advisers will be happy to assist you

Where to buy a Céline product?

Céline products are sold exclusively within our own store network around the world. You can visit our website to see the full list of our stores with opening hours and contact details at Céline.com in the STORES section.

How to maintain Céline products?

You will find all of the maintenance recommendations in each product page through the product care link.

Where to have Céline products repaired?

Céline products are made to the highest specifications and should provide clients with many years of enjoyment. While we do not provide a leather goods warranty for our products, complimentary repair or alternate resolutions may be offered based on the specific assessment of your item if it displays an issue specifically related to the way it was crafted


STORE REPAIR

All of our Céline stores offer repairs, and our staff would be delighted to assist you with an assessment. Our trained Client Advisors will be able to give you the best advice and the related cost, if applicable, for the required repairs. The full list of our stores is available at our Store Locator.


SHIP YOUR REPAIR

If you are unable to take your item to a Céline store, do not hesitate to contact our Client Services at Céline. Our specialized advisors will be glad to provide you with all necessary information for sending your item to Céline for repair.

Is it possible to find Céline spare parts?

All Céline products are handmade and request the highest level of craftsmanship not only for production but also for repairs. In order to guarantee the quality of these repairs, Céline does not propose spare parts. If you require further information, please contact the repairs department of any Céline store or the Client Services at Céline.

BUYING ONLINE OR BY PHONE
Need advice? Information? Placing an order by phone?

Our advisors are at your disposal. Please contact Client Services at Céline from 8am - 9pm CT Monday through Friday. Saturday from 9am - 9pm CT, and Sunday from 10am - 9pm CT.

What is a My Céline account?

Creating a My Céline account will enable you to:

Follow your online orders, access your purchase history and e-receipts

Manage your personal information

Opt-in/out of Céline Digital Communication

Save your personal wishlist for access on any device

How to order online?

Once you have chosen your products, add them to your cart by clicking on "Place in Cart".

The order process has 4 stages:

Register by creating your own My Céline account, using your email address and password. You can use this for future orders, to review previous orders, and to save items to your wishlist.

Fill in the delivery information: receiver’s name and address and select your delivery method.

Complete the payment information.

Review your order and submit it.

How to make payment?

Payments are accepted via credit card, debit card, PayPal, and Apple Pay with a valid United States or Canada billing address.

Accepted credit cards: Visa, American Express, MasterCard, and Discover

Payment Security

All transactions are secured. The Céline website is provided with an SSL encryption system to protect personal and payment data.

Céline does not keep any credit card information.

SHIPPING & DELIVERY
How can I track my order status?

Once your item(s) have shipped you will receive a confirmation email where you will find a link to track your order. In order to check the status of your shipment just click on the link, here you will also have the option of opting in for text updates. You will receive an email confirmation once your order has been delivered.

If you need further assistance tracking your package, please contact FedEx directly at 1-800-GO-FEDEX and provide your tracking number.

Is a signature required upon delivery of my order?

For security reasons, an adult signature is required upon delivery. If you will be unavailable to sign for your package, Contact Us and a Client Advisor will arrange to have your order held at a nearby FedEx location for pickup.

FedEx will make a total of three delivery attempts on three consecutive business days. After the third attempt, your order will be returned to our warehouse.

Are there any delivery restrictions? Can I ship internationally?

The following delivery restrictions apply:

  • Purchases may not be shipped to P.O. boxes, APO/FPO addresses, hotels, freight forwarders or to any address outside of the United States. To bill an order to your APO/FPO address, please Contact Us.
  • Items purchased from the U.S. Gucci website may only be shipped within the continental U.S. and to Alaska, Hawaii and Puerto Rico. If you would like to ship an order internationally, you may shop from that country’s website or Contact Us and a Client Advisor will assist you.
  • Items can only be shipped to one address per order. To ship to multiple addresses, please place a separate order for each location.
  • Orders cannot be shipped to a Gucci store for pickup.
  • Due to shipping restrictions from the U.S. Department of Transportation, fragrances may not be shipped by air. Please allow for additional time for delivery of these items.
  • Orders containing fragrances and décor products may be delivered only within the continental U.S. and cannot be delivered to Alaska, Hawaii or Puerto Rico.
  • Python and crocodile products may not be shipped to California.
Can I modify my shipping address?

If you need to modify your shipping or delivery information after your order has been placed, please Contact Us as soon as possible. Shipping information, including delivery address, cannot be modified once the order has already been shipped.

Please note, shipping and delivery information for orders purchased through PayPal and Amazon Pay may not be modified once the order has been submitted.

QUESTIONS ABOUT CÉLINE ?
What is Céline doing to combat counterfeiting?

The fight against counterfeiting is a long-term element in Céline's worldwide sustainability strategy. Céline believes that it is essential to preserve the house's ancestral know-how and the work of its craftsmen by fighting the illegal networks that infringe on human rights, the environment and global economy.

Céline has created a specific team fully dedicated to the management and protection of intellectual property rights (trademarks, designs, models, domain names, copyrights) based in Paris with regional offices in Tokyo, Hong Kong, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Milan, New York, Dubai and Buenos Aires.

In 2010, Céline initiated 10,673 raids (43 per day) and 30,171 anti-counterfeiting procedures worldwide, resulting in the seizure of thousands of counterfeit products and the breaking up of criminal networks. 1250 litigious websites were shut down in 2010. Céline guarantees authenticity by means of an exclusive distribution network; Céline products are only sold in Louis Vuitton stores and through the website Céline.com
