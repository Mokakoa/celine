About Us

Inspired by timeless staples, practical comfort, and soothing tones; Céline believes that our products are an investment for your life and should compliment you in all things. With quality and craftsmanship at the heart, Céline keeps a strong focus on production and hand-selecting sustainable pieces from emerging designers, local artists, and bigger brands alike. Céline design for women who live by that standard — those who make everyday beautiful.

About Us

Inspired by timeless staples, practical comfort, and soothing tones; Céline believes that our products are an investment for your life and should compliment you in all things. With quality and craftsmanship at the heart, Céline keeps a strong focus on production and hand-selecting sustainable pieces from emerging designers, local artists, and bigger brands alike. Céline design for women who live by that standard — those who make everyday beautiful.

Meet The Founder

"I am Luisa Grahams. I was highly inspired by the women's beauty. From Mies van der Rohe's works to my design, I acquired a rationalist vision of design by gleaning how they served a utilitarian purpose in a cleverly simple way. Céline merges elegant design and fashion, creating functional products made of luxurious and honest materials to improve people's lives in small but important ways."

In Production

#01 In Studio And Factory

  Design Area Studio in Roma

In a small studio in Roma - where we create all things that make you be more beautiful. With our love and thoroughness, we believe you will be satisfied.

  Made-to-wear Factory in Venice

Our made-to-wear factory is a small and intimate factory with around 200 skilled tailors, located just half an hour warehouse in Venice, Italy. By shortening the transit distance, we minimize the environmental impacts and price costs in supply chain logistics.

#02 Interior Workplace

We issue a strict compliance audit to ensure high employee satisfactions and environment standards. Staff members work in a highly interior workplace with the thoroughness and meticulousness on each product. We ensure all products are both perfect and beautiful.

We regularly visit our factory to ensure suppliers are running under our ethical workers standards: fair wages, employee's welfare, weekend reinforcement and community benifits.

Based in the heart of Italy, all products are manufactured by family-owned factory in Venice.

#03 Quality Promise

Quality is never an accident. It is always the result of intelligent effort. We spend most of our time and energy for good design and to achieve high quality. Every single detail from material to technique is thought through with obsessive attention. If our product doesn't satisfy you, we'll take it back.

"Signature" The dash in Céline’s logo is a reference to the hand-sewn signature stitch found on every product, and is a symbol of our commitment to superior quality in all aspects of our business.

"Signature" At the core of our mission are an unwavering standard of design, meticulous Italian craftsmanship, luxury-grade materials, and a transparent approach to the value and pricing of our product.
