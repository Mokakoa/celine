New Arrivals

For Céline's new arrivals, we celebrate quality—of life, of craftsmanship, of style. An ode to warmer days and brighter outlooks, the collection showcases a natural color and luxury leather material. From block-heeled loafers and open toe mules to leather ankle boots to barely-there sandals and unlined ballet flats.

The New Flats

Add direction to any ensemble with our signature pointed-toe styles. Schipper Rosie Flats bring this family structural elegance and simple, while elegant loafers and timeless mules redefine classic refinement.

The unlined flat with a sleek and minimal silhouette is endlessly versatile. Made in Italy from premium-grade leather.

Thelma Flat

The versatile Thelma flat is an easy slip-on shoe for fast-paced everyday life. This is an unlined shoe with a pointed toe that is flattering on the leg. The stitched seams at the front and sides create a modern, engineered finish.

Pre Fall '21 Collection

To be still and alone is to allow the immensity within ourselves to swell to the surface. Our Pre Fall ’21 Collection takes its cue from the intimate act of introspection. As we let the changes and challenges from the past five years sink in, we create an inner space to contemplate our journey.

The Best Look

With this collection, we concisely map out our inner worlds onto pieces old and new, revealing the undercurrents of who we are and what’s to come.

New sharp pointed-toe shoes in monochrome make their debut along with sleek square-toe styles in classic colors and luxe snake prints, relaxed and refined moccasins, and beloved cowboy styles with a suede twist.

Renee Satin Leather Boots

For transitioning from season to season, Renee Satin Leather Boots is an easy choice. Understated and polished, this new pointy ankle boot with a miniature cigarette heel that will take you into fall in style.
